# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

Argument Architect exists to help learners and teachers. We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful, welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background, experience level, or identity.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, pull requests, and discussions.
- Assume good faith. Many contributors are educators or students, not professional developers.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Give credit where it is due, especially when a contribution builds on someone else's idea.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or imagery.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Sustained disruption of discussions.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please open a confidential report by emailing the maintainer through the contact linked on the author's GitHub profile. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct, and may temporarily or permanently ban contributors for behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, or harmful.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted in spirit from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
